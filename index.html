Open

Welcome to the LibLogicalAccess project web page. LibLogicalAccess is a free and open source RFID library (LGPL license) developed in C++. It provides a good abstraction layer that lets you use different cards and reader in a semantically consistent way.


Portable

LibLogicalAccess is designed for portability. The library works well on both Linux and Windows, as well as Mac OSX. The codebase also build and works on both x86/amd64 and arm.
On Windows, you can purchase the appropriate component to develop directly using your favorite development language (C# .NET, Delphi ...).

Good hardware support

LibLogicalAccess architecture is modules based. This allows it to support various card readers and RFID chips in a flexible way.

In short, it works with most PC/SC, NFC and proprietary-based protocol readers for main RFID chips on the market (Mifare Classic, Mifare Plus, Mifare DESFire EV1, Mifare Ultralight C, HID iClass, HID Prox, Sony FeliCa ...).
